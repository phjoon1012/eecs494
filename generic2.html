DemonSlayer Simulator

This is a game inspired by the animation "Demon Slayer"

Altough I put a lot of effort to resemble the atmosphere of the animation "Demon Slayer", the focus of this project is to gain experience in iterative game design in which I develop the most challenging aspect of a new game in simple manner and get user feedback to improve further

My original mechanic I bring to this game was Move-Attack mechanism in which the attack and movement are hard coupled to one another, making the player to risk collision with the enemy in attempt to kill one.

The feedback I got the most was that there is a gap of thinking that I attacked the enemy, yet the collision happens first, making the game somewhat hard and illogical. Although the timing of attack is intended, I improved the collision mechanism significantly straightforward so that the player doen not feel that gap to provide more enjoyable experience
