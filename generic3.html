Final Project: B4 Last Shift

For the last project, me and my two other collegues worked on a 3D horror game that takes place in a parking lot. The protagonist attempts to complete his task as a parking duty while another mysterious entity is present.

This was definately the most challenging and rewarding project ever while I was at the university. I structured the parking lot and several other key features such as the mannequinn movement and level design.

I gained substantial knowledge in lighting, animation, and other components that were not addressed in 2D games.
